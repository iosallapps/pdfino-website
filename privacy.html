5.1 Advertising & Ad Personalization

For Free Users: We display advertisements through Google AdMob. These ads help us keep the app free for users who don't purchase a premium subscription.

Ad Personalization: When you first use the app, we'll ask your permission to use your Advertising Identifier (IDFA) to show personalized ads. You can:

  • Allow: See ads tailored to your interests based on your device's advertising identifier
  • Deny: See contextual ads based only on the current app content, not your personal profile
  • Change Later: Go to iOS Settings â†’ Privacy & Security â†’ Tracking to update your choice

Ad Attribution (SKAdNetwork): We use Apple's SKAdNetwork framework for privacy-preserving ad measurement. This technology allows us to measure ad effectiveness without sharing your personal information with advertisers. SKAdNetwork works entirely on-device and doesn't require tracking permission.

Premium Users: No ads are shown. If you subscribe to Pdfino Premium, all advertisements are removed and no advertising data is collected.
